About Us

We are researchers from the University of the Immaculate Conception that are tasked to create a portal for an Datasets Building and have an API Endpoint for each dataset for the endemic Birds found in Mindanao.

University Of the Immaculate Conception

What We Do

Purpose

Empowering other researchers to share their datasets to other researchers for future references.

Control

Allow the creators of datasets to manage their datasets while also allowing the back-end to deal with auto generated csv and json files, ready to download.

Scope and Limitation

The researchers of study have limited their research to endemic Birds. They are particular that they are only in the Mindanao Region.

Secure

A secure online repository with features that allow other researchers to get data from their chosen sets.

Access Point

To create a portal and api endpoint for other people to access to and or from.

Availability

Make datasets downloadable as JSON and CSV.
